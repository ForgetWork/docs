## Focus window actions

From the focus window, you can:

- Complete the current task
- View the time left
- Skip to the next task
- Go back to the previous task

## Why is this useful?

Tracking productivity is a great way to stay motivated and focused.

However, manually tracking your productivity is tedious and
time-consuming.

With focus time tracking, you can measure your productivity as you work.

Focus time can provide great insight when you use [Wrap day](/guides/wrap-day).

It can also be used to track and hit your [daily goal](/guides/daily-goal).

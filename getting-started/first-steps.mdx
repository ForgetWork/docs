## Your first session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Go to `https://forget.work` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first tasks">
    Create your first item (for example: a note, task, or reminder).

    <Tip>
      Keep it small: start with one thing you don't want to forget today.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize and sort them as you please">
    Drag n drop them in the order that makes the most sense for you!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make it a habit">
    Come back daily and add/close items so your list stays current.
  </Step>
</Steps>

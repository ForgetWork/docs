## Sign up

1. Go to `https://forget.work`.
2. Select **Sign up**.
3. Follow the prompts to create your account.

<Check>
  Once you're signed in, you're ready to follow the First steps guide.
</Check>

## Sign in

- If you already have an account, use **Sign in** from `https://forget.work`.

## Forgot your password?

Use **Forgot password** on the sign-in screen and follow the email instructions.

## Need help?

If you're stuck signing in, contact `support@forget.work`.
